Design a complete iOS 26 mobile flashcard app called "No Pressure Flashcards" with 16 screens in LIGHT THEME using modern iOS design with photo cards and glassmorphism.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
VISUAL STYLE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

- Clean white/light gray backgrounds (#FFFFFF, #F8F9FA)
- Bright blue #0A84FF as primary accent (iOS system blue)
- Large photo cards with lifestyle photography (mountains, cafes, study scenes, nature, landmarks)
- Warm, inviting photos with golden hour lighting
- Text overlays with dark gradients for readability
- SF Pro typography, modern iOS spacing and shadows
